Sanity Check

Automated Sanity Dashboard Check (ASDC)

ASDC is a comprehensive suite of automated tests designed to validate the functionality and performance of critical components in the e-commerce application. These tests are executed regularly to ensure that the application meets the specified requirements and maintains high standards of quality.

On-The-Fly (OTF) Testing

OTF testing involves ad-hoc testing of specific features or functionalities in the e-commerce application to validate their behavior and performance in real-time. Unlike predefined test cases, OTF testing allows testers to explore the application dynamically and identify potential issues or improvements on-the-fly.
