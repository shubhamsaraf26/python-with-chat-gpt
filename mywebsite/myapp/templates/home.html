Welcome to the Daily Sanity Checks

Daily sanity checks are essential to ensure the smooth operation and reliability of our e-commerce application. This page provides an overview of the sanity checks performed and their importance.

Purpose of Sanity Checks

The purpose of daily sanity checks is to identify any potential issues or anomalies in the e-commerce application before they impact the user experience or business operations. By performing these checks regularly, we can ensure that the application remains stable, secure, and fully functional.

Frequency of Checks

Sanity checks are performed daily, typically during off-peak hours, to minimize disruption to users. The checks cover various aspects of the application, including functionality, performance, security, and data integrity.

Types of Sanity Checks

The daily sanity checks include but are not limited to:

  • Verification of critical functionalities such as login, product search, and checkout process.
  • Monitoring system performance and resource utilization.
  • Reviewing error logs and system alerts for any anomalies.
  • Ensuring data integrity and consistency across databases.
  • Checking for security vulnerabilities and applying necessary patches.
